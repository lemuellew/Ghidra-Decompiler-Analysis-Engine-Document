\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
A p-\/code emitter for building \mbox{\hyperlink{classPcodeOp}{Pcode\+Op}} objects. 

The emitter is attached to a specific \mbox{\hyperlink{classFuncdata}{Funcdata}} object. Any p-\/code generated (by \mbox{\hyperlink{classFlowInfo}{Flow\+Info}} typically) will be instantiated as \mbox{\hyperlink{classPcodeOp}{Pcode\+Op}} and \mbox{\hyperlink{classVarnode}{Varnode}} objects and placed in the \mbox{\hyperlink{classFuncdata}{Funcdata}} {\itshape dead} list.
