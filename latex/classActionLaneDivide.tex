\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
Find Varnodes with a vectorized lane scheme and attempt to split the lanes. 

The \mbox{\hyperlink{classArchitecture}{Architecture}} lists (vector) registers that may be used to perform parallelized operations on {\bfseries{lanes}} within the register. This action looks for these registers as Varnodes, determines if a particular lane scheme makes sense in terms of the function\textquotesingle{}s data-\/flow, and then rewrites the data-\/flow so that the lanes become explicit Varnodes.
