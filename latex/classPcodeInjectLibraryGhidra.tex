\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
A p-\/code injection library that uses a Ghidra client to generate/compile the injection p-\/code. 

The \mbox{\hyperlink{classInjectPayload}{Inject\+Payload}} objects produced by this library are just placeholders (see \mbox{\hyperlink{classInjectPayloadGhidra}{Inject\+Payload\+Ghidra}}). At the time of injection, final p-\/code is generated by the Ghidra client.
