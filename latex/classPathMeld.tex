\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
All paths from a (putative) switch variable to the C\+P\+U\+I\+\_\+\+B\+R\+A\+N\+C\+H\+I\+ND. 

This is a container for intersecting paths during the construction of a \mbox{\hyperlink{classJumpModel}{Jump\+Model}}. It contains every \mbox{\hyperlink{classPcodeOp}{Pcode\+Op}} from some starting \mbox{\hyperlink{classVarnode}{Varnode}} through all paths to a specific B\+R\+A\+N\+C\+H\+I\+ND. The paths can split and rejoin. This also keeps track of Varnodes that are present on {\itshape all} paths, as these are the potential switch variables for the model.
