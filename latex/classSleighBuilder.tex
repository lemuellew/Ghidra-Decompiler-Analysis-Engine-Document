\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
Build p-\/code from a pre-\/parsed instruction. 

Through the build() method, {\bfseries{this}} walks the parse tree and prepares data for final emission as p-\/code. (The final emitting is done separately through the \mbox{\hyperlink{classPcodeCacher_a75c21a233c9eef255c53a498790882fc}{Pcode\+Cacher.\+emit()}} method). Generally, only p-\/code for one instruction is prepared. But, through the {\bfseries{delay-\/slot}} mechanism, build() may recursively visit additional instructions.
