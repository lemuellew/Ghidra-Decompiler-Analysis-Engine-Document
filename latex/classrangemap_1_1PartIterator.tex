class rangemap$<$ \+\_\+recordtype $>$\+::\+Part\+Iterator}

An iterator into the interval map container. 

This is really an iterator to the underlying multiset, but dereferencing it returns the {\bfseries{recordtype}}. Iteration occurs over the disjoint sub-\/ranges, thus the same {\bfseries{recordtype}} may be visited multiple times by the iterator, depending on how much it overlaps other {\bfseries{recordtypes}}. The sub-\/ranges are sorted in linear order, then depending on the {\bfseries{subsorttype}}.
