\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
Locate {\itshape shadow} Varnodes and adjust them so they are hidden. 

A {\bfseries{shadow}} \mbox{\hyperlink{classVarnode}{Varnode}} is an internal copy of another \mbox{\hyperlink{classVarnode}{Varnode}} that a compiler produces but that really isn\textquotesingle{}t a separate variable. In practice, a \mbox{\hyperlink{classVarnode}{Varnode}} and its shadow get grouped into the same \mbox{\hyperlink{classHighVariable}{High\+Variable}}, then without this \mbox{\hyperlink{classAction}{Action}} the decompiler output shows duplicate C\+O\+PY statements. This \mbox{\hyperlink{classAction}{Action}} alters the defining op of the shadow so that the duplicate statement doesn\textquotesingle{}t print.
