\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
Toggle whether a warning should be issued if a specific action/rule is applied. 

The first parameter gives the name of the \mbox{\hyperlink{classAction}{Action}} or Rule\+Action. The second parameter is \char`\"{}on\char`\"{} to turn on warnings, \char`\"{}off\char`\"{} to turn them off.
