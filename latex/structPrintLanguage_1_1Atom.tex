\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
A single non-\/operator token emitted by the decompiler. 

These play the role of variable tokens on the R\+PN stack with the operator tokens. The term {\itshape variable} has a broader meaning than just a \mbox{\hyperlink{classVarnode}{Varnode}}. An \mbox{\hyperlink{structPrintLanguage_1_1Atom}{Atom}} can also be a data-\/type name, a function name, or a structure field etc.
