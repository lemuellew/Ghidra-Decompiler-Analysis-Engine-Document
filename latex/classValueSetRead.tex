\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
A special form of \mbox{\hyperlink{classValueSet}{Value\+Set}} associated with the {\itshape read} {\itshape point} of a \mbox{\hyperlink{classVarnode}{Varnode}}. 

When a \mbox{\hyperlink{classVarnode}{Varnode}} is read, it may have a more restricted range at the point of the read compared to the full scope. This class officially stores the value set at the point of the read (specified by \mbox{\hyperlink{classPcodeOp}{Pcode\+Op}} and slot). It is computed as a final step after the main iteration has completed.
